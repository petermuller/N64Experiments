The Experiments

Beetle Adventure Racing (EA, 1999)

File: BARscoring.asm

Scoring Modification

With this hack, the scoring algorithm of the game is changed. Instead of abiding by the 2, 5, and 10 point box increments, all boxes use the following convention. If the current score is even and a box is hit, then the score will increment by 1. If the current score is odd, then the score will double.

Side effects

  • Boxes will no longer break, allowing point increments many times while the car is still in contact with the box.
  • Prolonged contact with the boxes will cause the score to increase indefinitely until the game crashes.

Other Notes

  • This was only tested in the Novice circuit tracks. This is not guaranteed to work in all races.
